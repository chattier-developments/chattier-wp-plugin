== Description ==

Chattier allows to generate intelligent chatbots for customer support. Chatbots can be easily inserted into any website.
Chattier supports standard text chatbots as well as 3D avatars with speech capabilities.

With this plugin, you can embed your Chattier chatbot or avatar directly into your Wordpress site without having to edit any code.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I configure my chatbot? =

After plugin installation, go to the settings section on your Wordpress dashboard and click on "Chattier Settings" page.

There you will find the steps you need to follow to embed your chatbot on your website. They are also described here:

1. Create your chatbot: Go to chattier.dev and sign in with your email. Then click on the “MY CHATBOTS” button at the top right corner.
If you have not created a chatbot before, then click on the button “Create new chatbot”. Follow the instructions in order to configure
your chatbot. Remember to include the domain of your WordPress site in the “Allowed domains” section. Wait until your chatbot is created
and make sure you can see your chatbot in the dashboard.

2. Get your chatbot embed code: Click on the green “Embed on website” button. Copy the code that appears in the box. You can select and
copy the text or simply click on the small copy icon on the upper right corner of the box.

3. Paste the code in the box: Paste the copied code in the box at your WordPress admin "Chattier Settings" page. Then select where you
want to activate your chatbot. You can activate it only on the front page or on all pages. Finally, click on the "Save Changes" button.

That’s all! Your chatbot should now be embedded on your WordPress website and ready to use.
